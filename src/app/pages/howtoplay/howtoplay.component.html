How to enter the prize competitions

1. Pick the Competition you wish to enter.

2. Select the numbers you would like in the draw.

3. Complete the payment formalities.

4. Choose the correct answer from the question given before you checkout.

5. You will be emailed your number in that draw and also placed interactively onto the competition page against your assigned     number .

How the winner is picked and when the draws go live

1. Only after the timer on the competition is stopped will the draw go ahead. A live streamed video to our host will appear within ‘ten     minutes’ of the draw timing out. One random number will be picked out by our random number generator. And then WE HAVE A     WINNER!

Delivery to the winner and uploading winner pictures

1. The prize will be delivered to the winner within 5 working days from the time of winner announcement. Delivery is free of charge to     all United Kingdom addresses.

2. The Winning pictures will be uploaded within 7 days on the ‘Winners’ page.
